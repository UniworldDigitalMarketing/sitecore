Unique

When exploring the great sites of Europe, it isn’t often that the mode of travel rivals the destination itself. Yet this is more than true for Uniworld’s flagship fleet—our company-owned vessels are works of art designed to be as extraordinary as the places we visit.

Expertise

By maintaining full control over Uniworld’s entire nautical, hospitality, and culinary operations—and never outsourcing to third parties as most river cruise lines do—we can ensure a consistently exceptional experience for every guest on every departure.

Service

We are proud to be so widely renowned for our gracious hospitality. Among our many accolades, readers of Travel + Leisure magazine recognized Uniworld for having the “World's Best Service (River Cruises).” Our rigorous quality assurance program ensures that we employ only the crème de la crème, and many of our staff have been with Uniworld a decade or more.

Cusine

All onboard meals in Europe include a generous selection of complimentary craft and local beers, spirits, and distinctive wines selected by acclaimed sommeliers. Onshore, we provide tantalizing opportunities to experience the local cuisine, with home-hosted lunches, wine tastings, and excursions highlighting regional delicacies—such as apricot schnapps in Austria and unique wine vinegar aperitifs in Germany.

Immersive

We also offer a plethora of exclusive excursions—extra special tours and private events offered solely to Uniworld guests—that are the talk of the river cruise industry. These excursions are often once-in-a-lifetime travel experiences, such as cocktails with an Austrian princess or a behind-the-scenes tour of the Spanish Riding School in Vienna.

LUXURY

Your luxurious Uniworld experience continues onshore as well. We work exclusively with premium hotels to guarantee a seamless and equally exquisite stay for our discerning guests. Each hotel property offers sumptuous accommodations with superb amenities, delicious cuisine, and attentive hospitality.

Uniworld’s all-inclusive, multigenerational river cruises in Europe provide young guests with mind-expanding travel experiences and opportunities for families to spend quality time together. And no river cruise line does family-friendly travel better than Uniworld. In fact, Travel + Leisure magazine has named Uniworld the “Top River Cruise Line for Families” the past four years in a row.

Imagine venturing inside a centuries-old clock in France. Watching high-tech robots assemble German cars. Getting a mesmerizing close-up view of medieval armor. Or spending Christmas with dinosaurs (at an amazing natural history museum). Creative cultural experiences such as these are as educational as they are enjoyable, making for memories the whole family will cherish forever.
