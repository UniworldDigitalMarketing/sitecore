IItaly is one of the world’s most beguiling destinations, and Uniworld’s luxurious, all-inclusive cruise/tours showcase the very best of this extraordinary country. From iconic landmarks to authentic cultural encounters to incredible food and wine, this intimate journey embraces Italy’s irresistible la dolce vita. See it all from the perspective of our newly enhanced and expanded 2016 itineraries onboard the beautiful River Countess.

Select 2016 itinerary highlights include:

  • Milan city tour with visit to Leonardo Da Vinci’s masterpiece, The Last Supper
  • Delightful “hidden gems” such as Verona, literary home of Romeo and Juliet
  • Valpolicella wine estate lunch featuring tantalizing regional dishes (formerly an optional excursion, now included)
  • Unique culinary excursions, such as a Chioggia market visit with a fresh seafood lunch or a do-it-yourself mussel harvesting experience
  • Exclusive—and extremely rare—after-hours access to St. Mark's Basilica in Venice
  • Full-day “The Magic Islands of Venice” tour of enchanting Burano and Mazzorbo
  • Choice of full-day excursion to Bologna with a hands-on pasta-making workshop or half-day excursion to Ferrara
  • Exclusive “Do as the Locals Do” walking tour of Venice or “Venetian Painters at the Accademia” tour with art historian guide
