Feel like starting your Uniworld adventure a bit early? Want to linger a little longer after your cruise concludes? Our optional pre- and post-cruise extensions offer the perfect opportunity to lengthen your journey in luxurious Uniworld style. Our optional extension packages include stays in first-class hotels, airport transfers, and knowledgeable local experts who will personally introduce you to the must-see sites and hidden gems of your chosen destination. To help you plan your trip, we’ve created a pamphlet for every one of our optional extensions, with detailed day-to-day information and helpful resources. Please click on any of the links below to download information about extensions available on your specific itinerary.
